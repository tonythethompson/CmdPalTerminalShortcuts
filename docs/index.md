## What you can do

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Saved folder shortcuts</h3>
    <p>Keep your usual projects one search away in Command Palette.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Your terminals</h3>
    <p>Uses profiles already on your PC — Windows Terminal, WSL, PowerShell, and more.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Command on open</h3>
    <p>Start dev servers or scripts automatically when a folder opens.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Home keywords</h3>
    <p>Jump to a shortcut from the palette home screen without opening Quick Shell first.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Backup and import</h3>
    <p>Export shortcuts for backup or move them to another PC.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Local only</h3>
    <p>Shortcut data stays on your PC. No account required.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Requirements

- Windows 10 version 2004 (build 19041) or later — Windows 11 recommended
- [PowerToys](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/powertoys/install) with **Command Palette** enabled
